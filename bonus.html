The Wagering Requirement Explained

This is the single most important condition attached to any bonus. A wagering requirement (also known as a playthrough or rollover requirement) is a multiplier that dictates the total amount you must bet before your bonus funds (or the winnings from your free spins) are converted from "bonus money" into "real money" that you are free to withdraw.

Let's look at a practical example:

  • You claim a bonus and receive C$100 in bonus funds.
  • The bonus comes with a 35x wagering requirement.
  • This means you must place a total of C$3,500 in bets (C$100 × 35) before the bonus funds — and any winnings generated from them — are unlocked and transferred to your real money balance.

This may seem like a daunting amount, but it is accumulated over many spins and is a standard practice in the industry. Every bet you make on an eligible game chips away at this requirement.

Maximum Cashout Limits

This condition is especially common with no-cost offers, such as no-deposit bonuses or free spins. A maximum cashout limit sets a cap on the amount of real money you can actually withdraw from winnings generated by a bonus.

The platform uses this rule to reduce financial risk on what is essentially a free offer.

Example:

If a no-deposit bonus comes with a C$100 maximum cashout limit and you win C$500 from your free spins, you will only be able to withdraw C$100 once the wagering requirements are met. Any winnings above the cap will be forfeited.

Game Contributions and Restrictions

When you are working to meet the wagering requirements, it is crucial to know that not all games will contribute equally to your progress. The bonus terms will clearly specify the game contribution percentages. The typical structure is as follows:

  • Most Slot Games: 100% contribution – every C$1 you bet counts as a full C$1 toward the wagering requirement.
  • Table Games (Blackjack, Roulette, Baccarat): Usually only 5%–10% contribution – every C$1 bet counts as just C$0.05–C$0.10.
  • Live Casino Games & Certain Jackpot Slots: Typically 0% contribution – these do not count toward wagering at all.

To meet the wagering requirements in the most efficient and effective manner, you should always focus your play on eligible slot games that offer a 100% contribution rate.

Time Limits and Expiry Dates

Bonuses and promotional offers are not valid indefinitely; they come with strict time limits that must be respected. There are usually several time-based conditions to be aware of. First, you will have a certain number of days to claim an offer after it becomes available. Second, the bonus itself (the free spins or bonus credits) will typically need to be used within a short period after being credited to your account (e.g., 24 hours to 7 days). Finally, and most importantly, you will have a set amount of time (e.g., 7 to 30 days) to complete the full wagering requirements on any bonus funds or winnings. If you fail to meet these requirements within the specified timeframe, the bonus funds and any associated winnings will be voided and removed from your account.
